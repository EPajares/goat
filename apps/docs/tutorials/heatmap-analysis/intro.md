# Introduction

Learn how to create powerful gravity-based heatmaps to visualize accessibility across your entire study area.

## What You'll Learn

Heatmaps show accessibility levels across an entire area, not just from specific points. In this tutorial, you'll:

- Understand gravity-based accessibility modeling
- Configure heatmap parameters
- Interpret and style heatmap results
- Compare accessibility scenarios

## Why Heatmaps?

While catchment areas answer "Where can I reach from here?", heatmaps answer **"How accessible is every location in my study area?"**
