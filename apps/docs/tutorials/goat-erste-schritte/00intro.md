# GOAT First Steps

## Introduction to Spatial Analysis with GOAT

Welcome to GOAT, the web-based GIS platform for data-driven urban, transport and environmental planning.

GOAT helps you analyze accessibility, calculate indicators, and compare scenarios ‚Äì all in a collaborative, web-based environment. From initial questions to meaningful maps, GOAT supports informed planning decisions.

## What Will You Learn from This Tutorial?

After completing this tutorial, you will be able to:

- create your own GOAT projects
- add and manage data
- apply key indicators and tools
- interpret and share results as maps and dashboards

This tutorial is practice-oriented: you'll learn not only where to click, but why certain analyses make sense.

## Learning by Doing: Supermarket Accessibility in Mannheim

As a comprehensive example, we'll analyze how well residents of Mannheim can reach supermarkets on foot.

You will learn:
- to calculate accessibility
- to interpret catchment areas
- to visualize differences between neighborhoods
- to make results useful for planning decisions
  
## What You Need

Before you start:
- A GOAT account ([register here](https://goat.plan4better.de))
- Interest in spatial questions
- ~45 minutes time

## Who Is This Tutorial For?

This tutorial is aimed at:
- Urban, transport and environmental planners
- Public authorities and consulting companies  
- Researchers and students working with spatial data
- Anyone with basic GIS interest who wants to apply accessibility-based analysis in practice

The focus is on planning logic, interpretation and application ‚Äì not on technical GIS theory.
